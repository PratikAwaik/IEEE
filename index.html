Message From Branch Counsellor

It is a matter of pride to pen down a message for the students who are willing to join the student branch of VGEC IEEE. IEEE is a pavement of learning technical abilities and making a professional prole about your accomplishments. You will get the opportunity to meet and learn from the fellow students, faculty member, Brainy scholars and professionals in the eld. You will get a chance to be part of workshops, hands-on sessions and webinar in order to acquaint the students with recent trends and development in technology. IEEE encourages the environment of experimentation and novelty for a beer cause. IEEE is a platform for students to express the technical pursuit which develops in their originality of thought and perception.

- Dr. Hiren Mehta

(Branch Counsellor, IEEE VGEC SB)

About

We are the student branch for IEEE, the world's largest organization for technical professionals, at VGEC. IEEE VGEC Student Branch gives students the opportunity to meet and learn from fellow students, as well as faculty members and professionals in the various fields.

Join IEEE

No matter what your background, if you are a student at VGEC, you are eligible for active IEEE student membership or you can join as a volunteer also. IEEE has ambitious students as it's members as well as faculty members passionate about spreading knowledge.
